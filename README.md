## Overview

<a href="#">Resident Evil</a> delivers survival horror through resource scarcity, atmospheric tension, and strategic combat. The application serves gamers appreciating horror experiences emphasizing vulnerability over power fantasy.

Enemy variety presents different threats requiring varied tactical approaches. Adaptive combat responding to <a href="#">enemy types</a> and behaviors maintains engagement throughout campaign.

Narrative progression reveals story through cutscenes, documents, and environmental storytelling. Character development and plot twists maintain engagement beyond mechanical gameplay keeping players invested in <a href="#">story outcomes</a>.

Checkpoint systems balance progress preservation with gameplay tension. Resume from reasonable points after failures without excessive repetition maintaining <a href="#">momentum</a> appropriately.

Audio design enhances horror atmosphere through directional sound. Hearing enemy positions and environmental cues through spatial audio creates <a href="#">immersive tension</a> effectively.

Graphics settings accommodate Mac hardware diversity. Adjustable visual quality matches system capabilities from integrated to discrete GPUs maintaining <a href="#">performance</a> across configurations.

Difficulty options cater to different skill levels and preferences. Challenge selection matches desired experience intensity from casual enjoyment through hardcore survival ensuring <a href="#">accessibility</a> appropriately.

Performance monitoring ensures smooth operation during intense sequences. Consistent framerate even during graphically demanding encounters maintains <a href="#">responsive gameplay</a> without disruption.

Save point distribution balances convenience against tension. Strategic placement requires progress risk assessment without excessive punishment maintaining <a href="#">engagement</a> balance.

Visual effects enhance combat impact and environmental atmosphere. Effects convey weapon power and environmental conditions without obscuring gameplay visibility maintaining <a href="#">clarity</a> during action.
